Akan Application

a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.

Girls Akan Names:Sunday: Akosua, Monday: Adwoa, Tuesday: Abenaa, Wednesday: Akua,
Thursday: Yaa, Friday: Afu, Saturday: Ama

Boys Akan Names:Sunday: Kwas, Monday: Kwadwo, Tuesday: Kwabena, Wednesday: Kwaku
Thursday: Yaw, Friday: Kofi, Saturday: Kwame
